Resources for Exercise and Fitness

Exercise and Fitness has so many different resources. There is so much available that it can often be overwhelming for someone wanting to start out. I find it most important to use the online resources to find what is best for your goals before starting.

Online Resources

  • Explore Apps: Wide selection of apps to choose from like Nike Run Club, PopSugarFit, and other free resources.
  • Online Exercise Programs: Many websites and apps offer various online exercise programs tailored to different fitness levels and interests.
  • Free Workouts: Several online platforms offer free home workout videos, including HIIT, strength training, cardio, and yoga.

Local Resources

  • Parks and Trails: Offer opportunities for walking, running, biking, and playing sports.
  • Community Centers: May provide fitness classes, gyms with equipment, and swimming pools.
  • Fitness and Recreational Facilities: Gyms, sports centers, and fitness studios offer a variety of exercise options.
  • Malls: Some malls have walking programs or open areas for light exercise.
